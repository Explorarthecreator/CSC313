BIOGRAPHY

Fred-Adeji Onose Emmanuel was born December 27, 1999. he hails from Uneme-Osu in Akoko-Edo Local Government in Edo State,Nigeria. Emmanuel is from a family of many people. He started his programming journey in 2018 of which he hopes to make progress and become one of the best in the field. Explorar, as he prefers to be called, started Explorar world with the aim of helping young ones like him on aspects that are of great importance to the society today, and hopes to reach a larger audience eventually.
